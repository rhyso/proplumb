Pro-Plumb are the main service agents for FERROLI Boilers and BIASI boilers in the South Wales area. Established 1990 and with over 25 years experience in the gas heating and plumbing industry we are in a very strong position to help you with all of your heating requirements.

Service, Install and Repair

In addition to the fact we are service agents for FERROLI and BIASI we also service, install and repair for all the major manufactures including WORCESTER, RAVENHEAT, ARISTON, VAILLANT, GLOWWORM, BAXI, IDEAL, HALSTEAD, VOKERA, SAUNIER DUVAL HEATLINE and many more.
